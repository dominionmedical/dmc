Measles notice | 麻疹通告

Measles is very infectious. The symptoms of measles include:fever, cough, runny nose, sore and watery ‘pink eyes’, rash. If you’ve got any of these symptoms, STAY AT HOME and call Healthline on freephone 0800 611 116 or the clinic. Vaccines are currently reserved for non immunised children only.

麻疹的症状包括高烧，流鼻涕，咳嗽和红肿的眼睛，然后在您的耳朵后面开始出现皮疹，几天后又在您的身体周围散布。 如果您认为自己有麻疹，请留在家里然后打电话通知诊所或 Healthline 0800 611 116. 麻疹疫苗目前只提供给未免疫儿童。
