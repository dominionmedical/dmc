如果您担心自己患有冠状病毒并有以下症状,请去检测站进行测试,8am – 4pm

  1. 流感样症状-发烧（体温至少38度）或任何咳嗽，呼吸急促和喉咙痛，以及在发病前14天内往返（不包括机场过境）相关国家或地区。
  2. 流感样症状-发烧（体温至少38度）或任何咳嗽，呼吸急促和喉咙痛，并且在发病前14天与可疑，可能或确诊的COVID-19病例密切或偶然接触 。
  3. 中度或重度社区获得性肺炎的医护人员。

Go to the testing stations, open 8am –4pm for testing if you have the following symptoms:

  1. Flu like symptoms – fever (temperature at least 38C) OR any cough, shortness of breath, and sore throat AND travel to or from (excluding airport transit) countries or areas of concern within 14 days before onset of illness
  2. Flu like symptoms – fever (temperature at least 38C) OR any cough, shortness of breath, and sore throat AND have been in close or casual contact with a suspect, probable or confirmed case of COVID-19 in the 14 days before onset of illness.
  3. Healthcare worker with moderate or severe community-acquired pneumonia
